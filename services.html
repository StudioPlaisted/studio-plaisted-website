Every project is a conversation between ambition and reality

We offer two pathways into working together — a standalone concept package for clients seeking early clarity, or full architectural services from first conversation through to handover.

Standalone Package

Concept + Costing Package

A standalone, fixed-fee service for clients who want a clear design direction and reliable cost information before committing to a full architectural engagement. The package delivers a builder-ready concept design with enough detail to brief a builder and begin initial cost conversations, giving you confidence and clarity early. It is a complete deliverable in its own right, and a natural starting point for clients who choose to continue into the full design and documentation process with the studio.

Full Architectural Services

Discovery

An initial conversation to understand your project, values, budget and ambitions. We establish whether there is a good fit and outline the path ahead.

Feasibility + Masterplan

A strategic assessment of your site’s potential, exploring what is possible within planning, budget and site constraints. For larger or more complex sites, this extends into masterplanning to establish a clear framework for development.

Concept Design

The design idea takes shape. We explore spatial arrangements, form, materiality and the relationship between building and site, establishing a strong concept that will guide the project through every subsequent phase.

Design Development

The concept is refined and resolved in detail. Structure, services, materials and finishes are coordinated, and the design is tested against budget and program to ensure alignment before documentation begins.

Development Application

Preparation and lodgement of all documentation required for planning approval, including liaison with local authorities and relevant consultants throughout the assessment process.

Procurement

We assist in selecting the right builder for your project through a competitive tender or negotiated contract process, ensuring the project is set up for a successful build.

Construction Documentation

Comprehensive detailed drawings and specifications that translate the design intent into buildable information, coordinated across all consultant disciplines.

Construction Administration

Ongoing oversight during construction to ensure the design is delivered as intended. Regular site visits, progress assessments, and management of variations and queries as they arise.

Handover

A thorough review of the completed project, ensuring everything meets the standard expected before you move in and start living in it. We also coordinate professional photography of the finished project to document the work and celebrate the outcome.
